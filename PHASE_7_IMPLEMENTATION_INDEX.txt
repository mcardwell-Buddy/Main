## ‚öôÔ∏è Execution Classes Explained

### AI_EXECUTABLE
- **Meaning**: Can be done entirely by AI
- **Human Intervention**: None needed
- **Blocking**: No
- **Effort**: 0 minutes
- **Examples**:
  - Extract records from database
  - Parse JSON and categorize
  - Validate email addresses

### HUMAN_REQUIRED
- **Meaning**: Must involve human judgment/action
- **Human Intervention**: Always needed
- **Blocking**: Often blocks AI
- **Effort**: 5-60 minutes
- **Examples**:
  - Review proposal and approve
  - Contact stakeholder for feedback
  - Sign off on compliance

### COLLABORATIVE
- **Meaning**: Requires both AI and human
- **Human Intervention**: Required for decisions
- **Blocking**: Depends on action type
- **Effort**: 15-45 minutes typically
- **Examples**:
  - Extract data, user reviews results
  - AI prepares, human makes decision
  - Process data, then get approval
